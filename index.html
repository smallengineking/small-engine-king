Our Story

Small Engine King is built around hard work, practical repair, and giving used chainsaws a second life.

Small Engine King is built on hands-on repair, honest sales, and helping buyers get dependable chainsaws without paying brand-new prices. We specialize in cleaning, inspecting, repairing, and preparing used chainsaws for resale.


We focus on clear listings, real photos, and practical equipment people can actually use. Some buyers want a solid work saw. Others want a project machine, parts unit, or vintage restoration candidate. Our job is to present each item as clearly and honestly as possible.


We take pride in giving equipment another chance to get back to work. That means real labor, real inspection, and direct communication with buyers before and after the sale.
