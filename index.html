About Small Engine King

Small Engine King is built around a passion for chainsaws and small engine equipment. We source, inspect, and prepare used equipment for buyers who want dependable machines without new-equipment pricing.

Whether you are looking for a work saw, a project saw, or quality used parts, our goal is to provide honest value, clear descriptions, and a smooth buying experience.

What you can expect

  • Clear product photos and detailed listing descriptions
  • Used equipment represented as honestly as possible
  • Quick order handling and professional packaging
  • Direct access to current inventory through our eBay store
