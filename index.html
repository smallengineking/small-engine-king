Our Story

Small Engine King was built around hard work, hands-on knowledge, and a focus on bringing used equipment back to life. We know that good equipment matters, and not everybody wants to pay new-unit prices when a properly serviced machine can still deliver strong value.

That is why we source equipment and parts, inspect what we can, refurbish what needs attention, and test units before they go out. We work to offer buyers equipment that is accurately represented, ready for the next job, and shipped out quickly.

Whether you are looking for a refurbished chainsaw, a usable power head, or parts for your next repair, we aim to make the process straightforward: honest listings, practical experience, and service you can trust.
